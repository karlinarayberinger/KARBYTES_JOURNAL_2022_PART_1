KARBYTES_JOURNAL_2022_ENTRY_27

The theme of the following journal entry is this: throughout my life (especially as an adult), I have noticed that other people seem to feel no guilt about being as cold, as withholding, and as blunt as I wish I could be (and feel that I really am inside but have been afraid to be because I have been conditioned from a young age to be disgustingly nice to everyone, to be a sycophant, and to let other people push me around like they are my dictators and, also, because I have long felt that there are not enough kind, friendly, and open minded people in the world and I felt that I am “naturally” more kind, friendly, and open minded than most people are in my midst). After I publish this note, I intend to be kind but not as accommodating of other people as I have been because I resent being in such a subordinate and weak position with respect to seemingly everyone else. I no longer wish to be so cuck like.

What spurred me to write this journal entry was spending enough time mulling over what has happened to my life since I split up with my first and last serious boyfriend (i.e. Spencer Eugene Dyer (whom I was with since 2009)) in 2015. Within a week of that breakup, I started casually dating a coworker from Lawrence Berkeley Lab whom I had a crush on (i.e. Alan Jason Ponte) after both of us were laid off from our jobs at LBL due to a lack of funds and a change of management in the LBL Information Technology Collaboration Services department. Alan got a new job and rarely was ever unemployed to my knowledge after he left LBL. I, on the other hand, only did short term menial labor jobs and mostly remained unemployed post LBL. Anyway, I wanted to focus on how my relationship dynamic with Alan has been: I seemed to always want more intimacy (physical and mental) with Alan than he wanted with me. He said that he was not really interested in dating anyone after we were “in a relationship” for no more than eight months. Around that time, my mind expanded greatly as a result of Alan introducing me to marijuana edibles, the Mandela Effect, Bernie Sanders, and other “consciousness raising” subject matter.

The highlight of our relationship, for me, was when we hung out with Alan’s friends in Berkeley and Oakland going to frat houses and house parties and restaurants. I felt like I belonged in his clique of bright acquaintances until Alan and I broke up in April 2016 because, according to Alan, I was too stressful to deal with and Alan did not want to deal with the burden I was. That 420, I spent alone in San Franciso going through a manic episode after consuming lots of marijuana edibles daily for weeks at a time. I thought that Alan and I were “meant to be” together for some divine purpose and that 2016 was some kind of new universe which split off from the universe which seemed to represent all histories prior to 2016. I thought I had to go on some kind of “rite of passage” quest to prove my bravery and competency to join Alan’s gang of magicians and visionaries (like they were some kind of modern Illuminati brigade with special powers and technologies the vast majority of human civilization at the time was oblivious to knowing existed). So I dropped a USB flash drive and a giant notebook full of my manic writings on the ground in the hopes that one of those “wizards” would find it, read it, and understand that I was ready to give up all my material possessions and devote myself 100% to joining their cause. When I got to the beach in Daly City after riding BART there, I abandoned my shoes and searched for Alan on the beach. I even asked random fishermen if they knew where Alan was. Once I got to a cave with some guys smoking weed in front of the entrance, I asked them if they knew where Jeremy Palmer was (the person who I thought was the elder of the group). The guys said he was up on top of the cliff. I looked up and saw some guy turn around quickly such that his red jacket billowed as he rotated. He was basically “the white rabbit” in this “Alice in Wonderland” trance dream play I was in. I continued venturing forth down a woodsy trail near Golden Gate Bridge and a girl on the trail said I am very brave. (At that time, I had the sense that every person knew who I was and what I was doing and that some kind of global event was occurring). As I traveled through the dark woods at night, I saw balls of light flashing here and there along the trail and one person sitting silently on a bench seemingly to make sure that I made it that far. After I made it out of the woods, I started running barefoot through some upscale neighborhood. It was silent. I seriously thought all the people died or else where abducted by aliens. Then I heard a girl singing but could not see her. I thought she might have also been singing to encourage me to keep going and to remind me that I am not alone. When I got to Golden Gate Bridge, I attempted to run across. That’s when the police detained me and brought me to the other side of the bridge inside of some trailer where they asked me what I was doing. I told them I was trying to find Jeremey Palmer and Alan Ponte. Then I was put in some psychiatric ward in San Francisco for about a week. While I was there, other people flooded in and seemed to be in a similar kind of manic quest as I was. It felt like we were there to help free the prisoners and protect ourselves from being brainwashed and medicated against our will. My parents found out where I was and were allowed to pick me up and take me home. On the ride home, I called Alan on the phone and he told me he could not talk because he just got back from the dentist. Later, we ended up hanging out at his apartment in Oakland doing marijuana edibles and watching batman as we cuddled on the couch. That was all I wanted.

Years later, I have felt torn between leaving Alan because we did not get along and he had a way of being very neglectful towards me and continuing to reach out to him because I thought he knew what was going on behind the scenes better than everyone else I know (and most people in general). As the time wore on, we saw each other less and less and we acted more and more like closeted gay people who had to keep our one night stands a secret. He never let me see his friends of family post April 2016.

Anyway, in September 2018, I felt very bad about my situation because I had been struggling to keep a job as a fast food worker but ended up quitting or getting fired and I was in my room at my mom’s house trying to get back to the happy nerdy me who did JavaScript. I got so stressed out that my mom and I got into some kind of fight in which she said I was too mentally unstable to have a real career. She gleefully filmed a broken bowl which I threw at the tile floor of the living room fireplace. I thought she was going to use that footage to smear my reputation even more. I saw her as wanting to hold her own daughter hostage so that she could have someone to rot with. I got so angry that I did not seem to mind if I killed her. I threw a steel teaspoon at the back of her head. She screamed and then called the cops to have me taken to Eden Hospital to be evaluated, then to John George Psychiatric Hospital, and then to Santa Rita County Prison. Within a month I was released. (See “The Pink Bin” for details about the crimes I have been convicted of).

After that almost second degree murder I committed, my mom filed a restraining order against me going to her house (yet I was still allowed to borrow her blue Toyota Matrix to commute to work and such). One day, when I was clocked into work at Panera Bread in Fremont, I started hearing voices talking inside my skull and other people around me seemed to hear my thoughts (and those voices seemed to be able to immediately decode my verbal, visual, auditory, olfactory throughput (but not tactile)). People were talking about me as though I deserved to be ostracized, ridiculed, and psychologically tortured. I have been in communication with those voices since 13_FEBRUARY_2019 and I suspect that Alan had something to do with it. I think he may have sedated me and installed something inside my skull while I was at his apartment in Oakland such that he and I were telepathically conjoined. Every time I have attempted to ask him if he did it, he denied that he did. I still suspect he has something to do with it and that he is one of the parties on the other end of this mysterious telepathic phone call. I think Alan wants to micromanage me from a good safe distance and act indifferent to me up close. I suspect that he wants to act like a tyrannical prison guard and prevent me from pursuing my interests while he pursues his as punishment for my crime in 2018. Things Alan have said to me over the years make me suspect that he condones retribution and treating violent criminals as harshly as possible because it is the only way for them to learn how to behave correctly.

I noticed that, when I have something to say or need information from Alan, he does not pick up the phone nor answer any of my messages when I send them to him. Perhaps he has “too much to hide” from me and wants to ghost me (i.e. abruptly stop talking to me without telling me he is done with me in this lifetime). Perhaps I should ghost him back and assume that is what is going on. Perhaps that is the only way to end the telepathic phone call (or to at least elevate the subject matter discussed within it).

Goodbye to Spencer and Alan; the people who have apparently ghosted me. I will stop reaching out to you people even if you reach out to me because I do not like “bait and switch” and “cat and mouse” games which waste time and cause depression.
