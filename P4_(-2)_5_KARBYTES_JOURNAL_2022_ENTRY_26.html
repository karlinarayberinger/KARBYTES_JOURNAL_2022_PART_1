It is almost 10PM and I am sitting in a cozy and secluded shelter up in the mountains to work on my laptop computer and write this journal entry.

I have been thinking about the “private” battles I have been dealing with (especially during my recent three shroom trips which I took during the past two weeks). What I have been grappling with is an overwhelming and haunting sense of paranoia that other people (including former friends and family members) feel animosity towards me for not living the way they think I should have been and that animosity includes them wanting me to be locked up and psychologically tortured in some kind of prison or mental hospital as punishment for my “bad” behavior, failure to conform to what they think is normal and “good” and “mandatory” behavior, and because they are jealous of the fact that I have been able to live so well and so selfishly while those people have committed themselves to working hard at whatever jobs they have done and relationship situations they have participated in while I mostly avoided socializing and mostly stayed unemployed (or “underemployed”) during the past decade.

I told myself while walking down the hill towards Lake Chabot that I do not want to befriend anyone while I think many people want me to be less healthy than I am, less happy than I am, less free to spend my time as I please than I am (most days of the week), and less focused on being an introvert with what they may think are obscure, silly, or uninteresting interests. I was afraid that most people want me to feel deprived of solitude and privacy, deprived of the ability to focus my attention on hobbies I care about, and generally treated in a cold, condescending, and confrontational manner by other people in order to discourage me from being the iconoclast I am and to terrorize me into molding myself into what they seem to want me to be.

I really do not think most people are “out to get me” as intensively as I describe, but sometimes certain kinds of people (especially strangers) seem to stalk me and wait to antagonize me in public to the extent that I feel like they are trying to get me to fight with them so that they can call the police on me and have me arrested. So I try to drown people out in case that happens by listening to loud distorted binaural beats via over ear headphones for hours on end when I expect to be within hearing range and within line of sight of such people. I wear sunglasses to hide my worried eyes which might even have tears in them. I sometimes wear a mask just to hide my mouth because I cannot help but express how anguished, angry, and even giddy I am a lot of the time when I am “performing under pressure” in the midst of those kill joys.

It has been suggested to me that I hate women. While I do not think all women are bad and I think men and women are almost exactly equal in terms of intellectual capacity, I do strongly believe that most women throughout most of human evolution have been sexually selected and culturally groomed to live in terror of being beaten up, raped, and murdered by men who acted as the captors and owners of those women. I seriously believe that women, throughout most of human history, have been treated like hostages of a patriarchal society and used as sex slaves and birthing cows against their will while their education mostly amounted to being trained to tolerate such subordination as inevitable.

Why would I hate women? Are they or have they been complicit in their subordination? I hate how mentally (and even physically) stunted women have been bred to be. I do not find it cute, attractive, or admirable that women normally seem to act like overgrown children who cannot transcend preoccupation with banal concerns because they subconsciously (or not so subconsciously) think that they will be neglected, abused, or otherwise disenfranchised if they do not constantly ingratiate themselves to whoever Big Daddy is. If I think a woman is acting unnaturally demure, dumbed down, and physically weak, I have a hard time feeling anything but pity and disgust towards her. I think there is a widespread “disease” amongst women which makes them settle for “misery loves company” as the only kind of company they are allowed to “enjoy”. Rather than pursue hobbies which make them feel happy to be alive, I think that many women would rather watch trashy television, gossip about other people (i.e. talk badly about other people in order to make themselves feel less pathetic), and wait for other people to “make the first move” rather than boldly venture forth ALONE to do what they are afraid to do but know in their hearts and souls is what is best for everyone (including themselves).

I cannot help but treat most women as mentally ill by default as a result of my experiences. My mother especially seems to have some kind of mental illness which makes her religiously sedentary and avoidant of brainy subject matter. I am afraid that both my parents are rotting rather than living and that they want to blame me for their lack of thriving. (Note that I do not think my parents are doing anything wrong. I just would not want to emulate how they live. When I say that I am worried that they are rotting rather than thriving, it is because they do not seem to get enough physical and mental exercise to be as healthy and as happy as they otherwise could be. The best I can do is set a good example and take care of myself and be in a good mood when I interact with them so that they see that I am well and happy rather than flailing or in distress). Why would they blame me? Because I still technically live with them (though I spend most of my time not with them and outside the house and do not tell them what my plans are most of the time) and because I still rely on them for economic support (including a daily allowance of approximately $40). To ease my sense that I am being grilled alive until I become 100% financially dependent of my parents, I am considering applying for part time blue collar jobs such as dish washing and cashiering if, for whatever reason, I am unable to get a better-paying job in the Information Technology industry. I have a sick feeling that most people will not be satisfied with me until I am working at least 40 hours per week doing a job which stresses me out yet pays for me to pay rent somewhere other than my parents’ houses (where my parents allow me to live rent free). I also think those people are seriously opposed to me getting by without a car. They seem to think that bicycles are for lazy kids rather than serious, hardworking adults. Apparently, I have to be ready to haul a bunch of stuff around and make it look like I am making all these sacrifices now so that, within a decade, I get married and have kids. If that is not my apparent agenda, then I apparently am not doing anything to serve humanity and I deserve to be locked up and tortured until I fall in line with the breeder agenda (which feels like being cornered, raped, and driven by terrorists who traumatize me into thinking and behaving exactly as they want me to: like a scared, helpless, eager to please authorities child who is good at pretending not to be distressed about these matters to the point that I seem oblivious to how unhappy I really am while others tell me trite advice about how I deserve to take an hour off constantly pleasing people each week to do my nails or something).

Until women (and humans in general) become a lot more educated, progressive, conscientious about the environment, interested in technological progress, and compassionate, I have concluded that most human relationships are a waste of my time and that it is best for everyone that I mostly keep to myself and treat people like mental ward patients learning how to move and think and speak on their own for the first time ever.

Thank you for reading this diary and taking the time to get to know me as a person better.
